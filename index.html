About Me

Full-Stack Software Developer

I am Antonio Jefferson owner of Prime Aspects, LLC a nationwide auto transportation company and Software Developer, residing in Clarksville, TN. I have a Masters of Science, Management degree, however, software development have been my passion ever since that old dial-up modem. After serving nearly 15 years in the United States Army working in the information technology and financial management fields, I am now living my dream as a software developer. During and after my time at Nashville Software School, I worked on projects as an individual and as a member of development teams. This experience not only helped strengthen my skills as a developer but it provided leadership, time management, and teamwork skills. During my personal time, I enjoy traveling, reading and volunteering at coding camps for children and young adults.
